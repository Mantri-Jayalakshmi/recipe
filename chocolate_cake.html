Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. your kids will love it. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation time Cooking time Total time
Easy 10 Min 25 Min 35 Min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and butter together.

2

 Mix the Sugar, Eggs, and the Flour.

3

 Mix every thing to get a soft and smooth dough

4

 Bake it in the oven
